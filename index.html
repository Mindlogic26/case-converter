What is a Case Converter?

A case converter changes how letters are capitalized in your text—for example, switching between sentence style, title style, or all caps—so drafts stay consistent with the tone and conventions of professional writing, from emails and memos to reports and presentations.

It also helps with data cleaning: pasted exports, CSV fields, and notes often arrive in mixed or accidental caps. Normalizing case makes values easier to compare, sort, and reuse without manual retyping in spreadsheets or databases.

For social media, quick case changes keep captions, bios, and hashtags readable and on-brand—whether you want emphasis in ALL CAPS, a softer sentence case, or polished title case for titles and headlines.
